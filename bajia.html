Bajia made with black eyed bean

    Ingredients
  • 2 cups Blacked-eye beans soaked overnight and drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloved of garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon cliantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powde
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
    Basbaas Sauce Ingredients
  1. 5 jalapenos or serrano/seeds temped
  2. 1 juice of lemon
  3. water
  4. salt

Instructions

Soak the black-eyed beans on cold water overnight. Drain the beans and remove the skin by rubbing the beans between your palms. Put the beans ina bowl filled with water and the skin will float on the surface. Get rid of the skin, put all the ingredients in a food processor and pulse in to a paste or grind them in to a paste, if it's watery add more flour, and if it's thick add more water. Place it in the fridge for 2 hours heat the oil in a pan on medium and fry spoonfuls of the mixture until golden brown serve with basbaas/green chili sauce as you like
